Website name: Squad Searcher

Idea: website to find players to play games with

Elevator Pitch: 
While gaming can be fun, playing by yourself can be boring from time to time. With Squad Searcher, 
players can find people to play with for a variety of different games.
Players can find team mates for different platforms and can search for 
people interested in ranked play or casual play.

League of Legends

Apex Legends

Overwatch
